FAQ & Troubleshooting

Quick answers to the questions that come up most — what's a demo account, why a number looks stale, and how to get notifications working.

Accounts & data

Demo vs Personal — what's the difference?

The Demo account is pre-loaded with realistic sample data so you can click around every screen without entering anything. Your Personal account is your real one — it starts empty and fills up only with what you add. They're completely separate; switch between them anytime from the account chip in the sidebar. When you're done exploring, clear the samples from Settings → Wipe demo data (it only removes demo rows, never your own).

Can others see my data?

No. This is a single-user dashboard — the data in your account is yours and yours alone. Your Personal data isn't visible to anyone else using the app.

How is net worth calculated?

Net worth is the sum of all your asset classes — stocks, mutual funds, gold, NPS, EPF, small savings, FDs, real estate, forex, chit funds, insurance value, and so on — minus your liabilities (loans and credit-card balances), all measured at today's live values. See Net Worth for the full breakdown.

Prices & the dashboard

My prices / net worth look stale.

Live values — stock prices, mutual-fund NAVs, gold rates — come from external market sources. They can lag when markets are closed (overnight, weekends, holidays) or during an occasional rate limit from the data provider. Nothing is broken — just reopen the page a little later and the latest values will load.

The Daily Digest shows no day-change.

The digest compares today against a previous snapshot. If you've never taken one, there's nothing to compare to yet, so the day-change appears blank. Take a snapshot first and the next digest will show the delta. See Daily Digest.

News headlines are empty.

The news feed occasionally goes unavailable upstream. When that happens the headlines section is simply blank — every other part of the page (portfolio, prices, action items) keeps working normally. Check back later and the feed usually returns on its own.

Notifications & AI features

Telegram isn't sending me anything.

Work through these, in order:

  1. Make sure Telegram is actually connected in Settings → Telegram notifications (the badge should read Connected).
  2. Use a new, dedicated bot created via @BotFather — not one you share with anything else.
  3. Double-check the bot token was pasted correctly (self-host).
  4. Confirm you pressed Start in the chat to finish the pairing.

Full setup walkthrough on the Integrations page.

Meal calorie / protein estimates are blank.

AI nutrition estimates are an optional feature. They only appear once you've added an OpenAI key in Settings → AI nutrition estimates. Without a key the Transformation tracker still works fine — it just skips the estimates. See Integrations to set it up.

How do I start the Transformation tracker?

It's off by default. Turn it on in Settings → Personal modules by clicking Enable — it appears in the sidebar. Then open it and click Start your 100-day reset. More on the Transformation Tracker page.

Tax & trust

Is this tax advice?

No. The app assists you in preparing your taxes — it organises deductions, computes estimates, and helps you compare regimes — but it is not professional tax advice. Always verify the final numbers against incometax.gov.in and consult a qualified professional before filing. See Income Tax.
