Getting Started

Artha is your single-screen command centre for investments, taxes, GST, and goals — all in Indian rupees, with live prices.

Signing in

Open the app and sign in from the login page. Depending on how your instance is set up, you'll either:

  • Pick an account — on a self-hosted instance you'll see two cards: Demo (a pre-loaded sample portfolio to explore safely) and Personal (your own empty dashboard). You can switch between them anytime from the sidebar.
  • Magic link — on the hosted edition, enter your email and click the one-time sign-in link.

Just exploring? Choose Demo first — every screen is filled with realistic data so you can click around without entering anything. Wipe it later from Settings → Wipe demo data.

Finding your way around

The left sidebar groups everything by what you're trying to do:

Sections collapse and expand — the one you're in opens automatically. Tap the account chip at the top to switch between Demo and Personal.

Key things to know

  • Everything is in ₹ (INR) and formatted Indian-style (lakhs/crores).
  • Live prices — stocks, mutual-fund NAVs, and gold update from market sources, so your net worth reflects today's values.
  • Inline editing — most detail pages have an Edit → Save toggle; you only change what you touch.
  • Financial year selector — the dropdown at the top-right of each page scopes tax and planning views to a chosen FY (e.g. FY 2026-27).

A good first 10 minutes

  1. Open the Net Worth dashboard to see your totals and asset mix.
  2. Add a holding or two — try Stocks or Mutual Funds — and watch the totals update.
  3. Set up your profile and assumptions in Settings.
  4. Connect Telegram to get a morning digest and alerts.
  5. If you file taxes here, start with Deductions and the ITR Wizard.
